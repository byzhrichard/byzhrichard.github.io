$\frac{0}{0}$型

1. 洛必达法则

对分子分母同时求导

  • 越求导越复杂的, 就不要继续洛必达了
$$ > \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{1-\cos x}{2x^2} > = \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin x}{4x} > = \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{(\sin x)'}{(4x)'} > = \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\cos x}{4} > = \frac{1}{4} > $$

2. 等价无穷小

当 $x \to 0$ 时:

  • $\sin x、\arcsin x、\tan x、\arctan x、e^{x}-1、\ln(1+x) \color{orange}\sim\color{black} x$
  • $1-\cos x \color{orange}\sim\color{black} \dfrac{1}{2}x^2$
  • $(1+x)^{\alpha}-1 \color{orange}\sim\color{black} \alpha x$

一般建议在乘除法里用

$$ > \lim_{x \to 0}\frac{x-\sin x}{x-x\cos x}=\lim_{x \to 0}\frac{x-\sin x}{\frac{1}{2}x^3}=\lim_{x \to 0}\frac{1-\cos x}{\frac{3}{2}x^2}=\lim_{x \to 0}\frac{\frac{1}{2}x^2}{\frac{3}{2}x^2}=\frac{1}{3} > $$

$\frac{\infty}{\infty}$型

1. 洛必达法则

略

2. 抓大头

只保留增长速度最快的项, 忽略其他项 (本质还是洛必达)

$$ > \lim_{x \to +\infty} \frac{x^2 + x + 1}{2x^2 + 3x} > = \lim_{x \to +\infty} \frac{x^2}{2x^2} > = \frac{1}{2} > $$
$$ > \lim_{x \to +\infty} \frac{x^2 + x + 1 + \ln x}{2x^2 + 3x + e^x} > = \lim_{x \to +\infty} \frac{x^2}{e^x} > = 0 > $$

$0 \cdot \infty$型

  • 转换成$\frac{0}{0}$型
  • 转换成$\frac{\infty}{\infty}$型
$$ > \lim_{x \to +\infty} \frac{1}{x} \cdot \ln(x^2 + 1) > = \lim_{x \to +\infty} \frac{\ln(x^2 + 1)}{x} > $$

$\infty-\infty$型

  • 转换成$\frac{0}{0}$型
  • 转换成$\frac{\infty}{\infty}$型

方法:

  1. 有理化 (适用于含根式)
  2. 通分 (适用于分式之差)
  3. 换元t (适用于既有x又有1/x)
$$ > \begin{align} > \lim_{x \to +\infty} \left(\sqrt{x^2+x} - x\right) > &= \lim_{x \to +\infty} \frac{\left(\sqrt{x^2+x}-x\right)\left(\sqrt{x^2+x}+x\right)}{\sqrt{x^2+x}+x} \\ > &= \lim_{x \to +\infty} \frac{\left(\sqrt{x^2+x}\right)^2 - x^2}{\sqrt{x^2+x}+x} \\ > &= \lim_{x \to +\infty} \frac{x}{x\sqrt{1+\frac{1}{x}}+x} \\ > &= \lim_{x \to +\infty} \frac{1}{\sqrt{1+\frac{1}{x}}+1} \\ > &= \frac{1}{2} > \end{align} > $$
$$ > \begin{align} > \lim_{x \to 1} \left( \frac{1}{1-x} - \frac{2}{1-x^2} \right) > &= \lim_{x \to 1} \left( \frac{1+x}{(1-x)(1+x)} - \frac{2}{(1-x)(1+x)} \right) \\ > &= \lim_{x \to 1} \frac{-(1-x)}{(1-x)(1+x)} \\ > &= \lim_{x \to 1} \frac{-1}{1+x} \\ > &= -\frac{1}{2} > \end{align} > $$
$$ > \begin{align} > \lim_{x \to +\infty} \left[(x+1) - x e^{\frac{1}{x}}\right] > &= \lim_{t \to 0^+} \left[\left(\frac{1}{t} + 1\right) - \frac{1}{t} e^{t}\right] \\ > &= \lim_{t \to 0^+} \left( \frac{1}{t} + \frac{t}{t} - \frac{e^t}{t} \right) \\ > &= \lim_{t \to 0^+} \frac{1 + t - e^t}{t} \\ > &= \lim_{t \to 0^+} \frac{0 + 1 - e^t}{1} \\ > &= \lim_{t \to 0^+} \left(1 - e^t\right) \\ > &= 0 > \end{align} > $$

$1^{\infty}$型

核心公式:

$$ \lim_{x \to \square} f(x)^{g(x)} = e^{\displaystyle \lim_{x \to \square} \big[f(x)-1\big] \cdot g(x)} $$

$$ \begin{align*} \lim_{x \to 0} \left[ \frac{\ln(1+x)}{x} \right]^{\frac{1}{x}} &= e^{\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0} \left[ \frac{\ln(1+x)}{x} - 1 \right] \cdot \frac{1}{x}} \ &= e^{\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\ln(1+x)-x}{x^2}} \ &= e^{\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\frac{1}{1+x} - 1}{2x}} \

&= e^{\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\frac{-x}{1+x}}{2x}} \ &= e^{\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{-1}{2(1+x)}} \ &= e^{-\frac{1}{2}} \end{align*} $$

$0^0$型

核心公式:

$$ \lim_{x \to \square} f(x)^{g(x)} = e^{\displaystyle \lim_{x \to \square} g(x)\cdot \ln f(x)} $$
$$ > \begin{align} > \lim_{x \to 0^+} \left(1-e^{-x}\right)^{\frac{1}{\ln x}} > &= e^{\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0^+} \frac{1}{\ln x} \cdot \ln\left(1-e^{-x}\right)} \\ > &= e^{\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0^+} \frac{\ln\left(1-e^{-x}\right)}{\ln x}} \\ > &= e^{\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0^+} \frac{\dfrac{e^{-x}}{1-e^{-x}}}{\dfrac{1}{x}}} \\ > &= e^{\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0^+} \frac{x e^{-x}}{1-e^{-x}}} \\ > &= e^{\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0^+} \frac{x e^{-x}}{x}} \\ > &= e^{\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0^+} e^{-x}} \\ > &= e > \end{align} > $$

$\infty^0$型

核心公式:

$$ \lim_{x \to \square} f(x)^{g(x)} = e^{\displaystyle \lim_{x \to \square} g(x)\cdot \ln f(x)} $$
$$ > \begin{align} > \lim_{x \to 0^+} \left( \frac{1}{\sin x} \right)^x > &= e^{\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0^+} x \cdot \ln \frac{1}{\sin x}} \\ > &= e^{\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0^+} x \cdot (\ln 1 - \ln\sin x)} \\ > &= e^{\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0^+} -x \cdot \ln\sin x} \\ > &= e^{\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0^+} \frac{\ln\sin x}{-\frac{1}{x}}} \\ > &= e^{\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0^+} \frac{\frac{\cos x}{\sin x}}{\frac{1}{x^2}}} \\ > &= e^{\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0^+} \frac{x^2 \cos x}{\sin x}} \\ > &= e^{\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0^+} \frac{x^2 \cos x}{x}} \\ > &= e^{\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0^+} x\cos x} \\ > &= 1 > \end{align} > $$
